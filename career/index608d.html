Digital Designer (Contract)

Email
Tweet
Location: Burnaby, British Columbia, Canada
Date Posted: 07-20-2018
Our client is one of the largest omni-channel retailers of consumer electronics, computers, and related accessories and services. They're a multinational retailer committed to growth and innovation with 100k employees across the United States, Canada, and Mexico.
 
As a Digital Designer you will have creative responsibility for revolutionizing our brand concepts to working digital creative features in an eCommerce space. You will work as part of the UX & Digital Design team to help maintain and evolve the company's digital presence. You have a keen sense of production design skills, you’re motivated, and passionate in what you do!
 
You are a strong team player and able to work in a highly collaborative, fast paced environment to meet tight deadlines and maintain quality and brand consistency.
 
Digital Designer (Contract)
Burnaby, British Columbia
 
What you’ll be doing:
  • Partner with cross-functional team to design, develop, and execute visual design standards and processes 
  • Drive the creative execution of User Experience deliverables and functional retail experience 
  • Conceptualize and build branded content to the digital space
  • Utilize technical skills to hand code HTML / CSS and modify HTML / CSS of existing templates
  • Utilize data and analytics to execute informed design decisions and improvements
What you bring:
  • A BFA in Graphic Design, Digital Interactive, Multi-Media, User Interface Design or other related field
  • 4+ years of experience in digital asset production, designing experiences across a variety of platforms and industries. 
  • Strong understanding of interaction design processes (from UX/UI, to design, to development)
  • Intermediate in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery, and Git
  • Intermediate with Sketch, InVision, and Adobe Creative Suite.
  • Understanding of branding, layout, color theory and typography in digital media
***An online portfolio is required for applicant consideration***
