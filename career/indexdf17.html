Our client, one of the largest and most successful home improvement retail companies in the world is looking to add a Manager of Online Product Management to their award winning e-commerce team! Recently named one of the world's most innovative companies this position will be responsible for driving consumer facing and backend projects for a specific product line on the companies website. 

Manager, Online Product Management (Site Search) 
Atlanta, GA 

What you’ll be doing:
  • Analyze the current customer experience and implement a roadmap to improve the online shopping experience for your specific product line. 
  • Feature development working directly with design and engineering teams.
  • Triage feature tradeoffs in relation to business needs, customer needs and added value.
  • Performance analysis of site features and enhancements. 
  • Regularly conduct competitive analysis on industry market trends. 
  • Partner with Key stakeholders to acquire buy-in and achieve cross functional support and calibration. 
What you bring:
  • 5+ years Product Management experience 
  • MBA or MS preferred  
  • Experience working in a consumer goods or retail company
  • Strong understanding of the Ecommerce industry
  • Experience with Adobe web analytics and/or Google analytics
  • Strong experience with MS Office Suite (Primarily Word, PowerPoint and Excel) 
  • Excellent communication (both verbal and written)
