VP of Operations and Manufacturing

Email
Tweet
Location: Vancouver, British Columbia, Canada
Date Posted: 06-13-2018
Our client is known for pioneering leading edge solutions and firsthand expertise in innovating what health means to the world.  At their core, they are spearheading innovation and aim to create a positive impact on a global scale. They are collaborating in the natural medicine, technology, hospitality and non-profit industries and are operating in Canada and the US. 
 
The team is looking to add a Vice President of Operations and Manufacturing (beverages and natural medicines) to be actively supporting corporate growth by establishing processes for outsourced manufacturing, purchasing, logistics, sourcing and R&D (chemistry). 
 
In this role, you will be providing leadership support for cross functional teams to ensure successful development of high quality branded products. You will be honing in on your skills in team leadership and maintaining a people-first approach when it comes to training and mentoring staff.

Vice President of Operations and Manufacturing
Vancouver, British Columbia

What you will be doing:
  • Hiring, training and mentoring team members in line with our people-first philosophy
  • Overseeing international supply chains, from purchase order to delivery
  • Setting key performance metrics
  • Leading team to achieve key metrics
  • Improving team efficiency
  • Managing expenses and revenue
  • Participating in planning, policymaking, and program development
  • Executing strategic initiatives, both corporate and local
  • Maintaining compliance with government regulations
  • Identifying and mitigating risks
  • Building external partnerships
