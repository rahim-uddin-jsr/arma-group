Senior Paid Search Account Manager
Full-time opportunities, El Segundo, CA

As a Senior Paid Search Account Manager you’ll directly manage search engine marketing (SEM) campaigns across both Google AdWords and Bing Ads, making optimizations and updates daily. Your complex and diverse portfolio of clients will be a mix of both big and small that constantly challenges you, and with extensive experience and/or a proven track record, you could be working with some of our biggest brands. You’ll be trusted to oversee all account activity, collaborate with managers from other channels, and come up with campaign strategies for your diverse book of clients.
 
What you'll be doing: 
  • Creating and managing PPC accounts/campaigns in Google AdWords and Bing Ads
  • Managing Display, Shopping, and Video advertising campaigns
  • Conducting keyword research, writing ad copy, and establishing bidding strategies
  • Analyzing SEM/PPC campaign results and adjusting strategy and budgets accordingly
  • Communicating with clients regularly to discuss goals and deliver results
  • Developing strategies across all marketing channels to improve ROI/ROAS and achieve maximum efficiency
What you bring: 
  • 3+ years of experience managing multiple client portfolios in the PPC Space
  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills
  • Deep knowledge of the SEM industry and the PPC advertising model
  • Experience with keyword research tools and web analytics tools (Google Analytics), as well as how to use these tools in SEM/PPC campaigns
  • General knowledge of search engine optimization (SEO), social advertising, and web usability
  • Experience working with third party bidding software, like Kenshoo or Double Click, are a plus
